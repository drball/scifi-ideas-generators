If you're looking for ideal names for spaceships, you're on ground well-trodden by the writers of Star Trek, which must have the largest list of named spaceships from any franchise! 

Below is a list of Federations starships (including non-Starfleet spaceships) used in canon Star Trek (so not including books or games). 

There's more information on <a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Federation_starships">this wiki page about Federation starships</a>, including links with information about each ship. A fun name on this list is the <a href="https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/USS_Alka-Selsior">USS Alka Selsior</a>, an an in-joke reference to the Alka-Seltzer brand-name of indigestion medicine, & a play on the name "Excelsior".
